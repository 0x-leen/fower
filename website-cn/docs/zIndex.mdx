id: zIndex
title: zIndex 层级
---

使用 `zIndex-{number}` 让您更加便捷的设置元素层级

## 用法

```tsx live
<Wrapper>
  <View s-100 bgBlue30 relative>
    <View s-50 center bgBlue50 absolute zIndex-1>
      big
    </View>
    <View s-30 center bgBlue70 absolute zIndex-2>
      small
    </View>
  </View>
</Wrapper>
```
