Styli 内置了阴影管理。

### 用法

```jsx live
<View bgGray10 p-20 centerX between>

  <View center c-50 bgWhite shadowXXS>
    XXS
  </View>
  <View center c-60 bgWhite shadowXS>
    XS
  </View>
  <View center c-70 bgWhite bgWhite shadowS>
    S
  </View>
  <View center c-80 bgWhite shadowM>
    M
  </View>
  <View center c-90 bgWhite shadowL>
    L
  </View>
  <View center c-100 bgWhite shadowXL>
    XL
  </View>
  <View center c-110 bgWhite shadowXXL>
    XXL
  </View>
</View>
```
